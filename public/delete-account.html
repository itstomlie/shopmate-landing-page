Delete your account & data

This page explains how to delete your account and personal data from Shopmate, the grocery receipt price-tracking app. Shopmate is developed and operated by the Shopmate team, contactable at support@shopmategrocery.com.

Delete in the app (fastest)

  1. Open Shopmate.
  2. Go to Account.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and personal data are removed immediately.

Request deletion by email

Prefer not to use the app? Email support@shopmategrocery.com from the email address on your account and ask us to delete it. We process email deletion requests within 30 days.

What is deleted

  • Your account and login credentials.
  • Your receipt photos.
  • Your receipt records and purchase history linked to you.
  • Your grocery lists.
  • Your profile.

What is retained

The prices extracted from your receipts are kept in anonymized form — detached from your account and identity — so the shared community price catalog stays accurate. These records contain no personal identifiers and can no longer be linked back to you.
