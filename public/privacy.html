Privacy Policy

Effective date: 5 July 2026

Shopmate ("Shopmate", "we", "us") helps you scan grocery receipts, track what you paid, and compare prices. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. Shopmate is operated from Australia; you can reach us any time at tom@shopmategrocery.com.

Anonymous by default. You can use Shopmate as an anonymous guest — no email, name, or account required. You only provide an email if you choose to create an account so your receipts follow you to a new device.

Information we collect

  • Receipt photos you capture or upload.
  • Purchase data extracted from those photos — store name, items, quantities, prices, and purchase date.
  • Email address — only if you create an account.
  • Approximate location — only if you grant permission, used to show nearby stores first when tagging a receipt.
  • Device & crash diagnostics — technical error and performance data collected via Sentry to help us fix crashes.

How we use your information

  • To build your personal receipt history and per-unit price comparisons.
  • To contribute the prices from your receipts, in anonymized form, to a shared community price catalog that helps all users find better prices. This catalog is not linked to your identity.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve the app.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your data and we do not show ads. We use a small number of trusted service providers to run Shopmate:

  • Supabase — hosts our database, authentication, and receipt-image storage.
  • Google (Gemini API) — your receipt image is sent to Google's Gemini AI service to extract the line-item data.
  • Sentry — receives crash and error diagnostics.

Data retention & deletion

You can delete your account and personal data at any time inside the app: Account → Delete account. You can also email tom@shopmategrocery.com to request deletion.

When you delete your account we permanently remove your account, your receipt photos, your receipt records and purchase history, your grocery lists, and your profile.

What remains: the prices extracted from your receipts are retained in anonymized form — detached from your account and identity — so the shared community price catalog stays accurate. These records contain no personal identifiers.

Location

Location access is optional and used only while the app is open, solely to sort nearby stores. You can decline or revoke it in your device settings without losing core functionality.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit. Receipt images are stored in a private bucket accessible only to you (and our server processes acting on your behalf). Access to your data is governed by per-user security rules.

Children

Shopmate is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children.

Your rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act and the Australian Privacy Principles, you may request access to or correction of your personal information, and you may lodge a complaint. Contact us at tom@shopmategrocery.com.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes we will update the effective date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.

Contact

Questions? Email tom@shopmategrocery.com.
